Let´s operate openly!

We come from building a network of operators who share knowledge on operating cloud & container infrastructure. But the topic is broader: we meet monthly with DevOps, Ops team members and all who are interested in automating and operating IT solutions in modern cloud environments. We want to talk about our practical approach, about best practices, failures and how to avoid them, setups, ... The more people are sharing their knowledge and experiences the better!

New CPU leaks

A new set of hardware CPU speculation vulnerabilities were uncovered in July/August 2023. They do create risks for information disclosure for providers and users of SCS clouds. This blog article explains the issues, the associated risks and how these can be mitigated by providers and users.
